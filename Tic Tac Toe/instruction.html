• Tic Tac Toe

Instructions

Game Board

  • The game is played on a 3×3 grid.
  • Each square can hold either X or O.

Players

There are two players:

  • Player 1 → X
  • Player 2 → O

Taking Turns

  • Players take turns marking an empty square with their symbol (X or O).
  • The game starts with X.
  • After every move, the turn switches to the other player.

Winning the Game

A player wins if they have 3 of their marks in a row, either:

  • Horizontally (across a row)
  • Vertically (down a column)
  • Diagonally (across the grid)

Draw (Tie)

If all 9 squares are filled and no one has 3 in a row, the game ends in a Draw.

Replay

Press the Replay button to reset the board and start a new game.
